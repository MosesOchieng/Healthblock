Welcome to a new era of health insurance services, where innovation and technology converge to reshape the way we approach coverage, protection, and financial well-being. In this dynamic landscape, we present a range of innovative health insurance solutions that harness the power of blockchain, cryptocurrencies, and smart contracts to offer you a transformative experience.

Communities Based Insurance.

This model involves creating insurance pools where users with similar health profiles and risk levels come together. Participants collectively share insurance risks, fostering a sense of community and mutual support.

Non Fngible Tokens Policies.

NFTs are unique digital assets representing ownership of insurance policies. Each NFT-backed policy can have customizable terms, coverage, and benefits. Users can personalize their insurance based on their needs, creating a more tailored experience.

Parametric Insurance Cover

Parametric insurance utilizes predefined parameters (such as weather conditions, seismic activity, or other measurable events) to trigger automatic payouts. Smart contracts are programmed to respond to specific triggers without the need for traditional claims processing. .

Micro-Insurance Cover

Micro-insurance provides coverage for specific health events or short-term needs. Users can purchase insurance for specific scenarios, such as travel or short-term medical care. It caters to individuals who require temporary coverage without committing to long-term policies.

Tokenized Insurance Cover

Tokenization involves representing insurance policies as tradable tokens on a blockchain. Policyholders can buy, sell, or trade these tokens, creating a secondary market for insurance. Tokenized policies offer liquidity and potential investment opportunities for policyholders.

Collaterized Insurance

Users can collateralize crypto assets to secure insurance coverage. Collateral provides an additional layer of security for both insurers and policyholders. Users can activate insurance coverage by locking collateral assets. In case of a claim, the payout is based on the collateral's value, enhancing trust and accessibility.
